 How many results (number of database records) can I request at once from my database?
  If your working on a local mysql server, you can try up to 5000, depending on number of fields in the table, total size of data.
If your searching a database on your remote web site, take into consideration the time it would take to display the 5000 results on a web page and divide by 2 or 3.
Its still allot of time, and although adminApp compresses the data, start off with like 50 or 100 results, and take it from there.
